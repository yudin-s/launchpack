# Recommendation

Recommended project: Worklog-to-Launch Kit.

## Positioning

"Turn messy founder work into a Product Hunt launch kit in 60 seconds."

## Why This Wins The Tradeoff

- Highest one-day feasibility: mostly text generation, templates, preview, and
  export/share.
- Strong Product Hunt fit: helps makers launch better, and PH users immediately
  understand the pain.
- Clear demo: paste raw notes -> receive launch page copy, maker comment, FAQ,
  posts, and launch checklist.
- Differentiated angle: not generic content generation; it turns real work into
  launch assets with provenance and confidence.
- Low integration risk: no live scraping, no auth-heavy APIs, no calendar or
  browser automation dependency.

## MVP Scope

- Input: paste notes, transcript, changelog, Git commits, or product brief.
- Analyze: extract product promise, audience, proof points, risks, objections.
- Generate: PH tagline, description, maker comment, FAQ, launch checklist,
  social posts, demo script.
- Preview: one polished launch-kit page with tabs/sections.
- Export: markdown and copy buttons.

## Stretch

- "Receipts" mode: show which generated claims came from which input snippets.
- Competitor angle prompt: compare against one named alternative.
- Landing-page hero preview.

## Do Not Build In Day One

- Product Hunt API posting.
- Multi-user accounts.
- Browser automation.
- Full CMS or database-heavy workspace.
- Video generation.
