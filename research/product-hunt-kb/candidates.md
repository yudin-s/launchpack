# Candidate Shortlist

## 1. Worklog-to-Launch Kit

Turn a founder/dev worklog, meeting transcript, or raw notes into a Product
Hunt-ready launch pack: landing copy, demo script, maker comment, FAQ, X/LinkedIn
posts, and a public changelog page.

- Impact: 5
- Novelty: 4
- Implementation: 5
- Risk: 2
- Demoability: 5
- Total: 4.65

Why it can work: combines the strongest signals from ProdShort/Velo/RankSpot but
keeps scope text-first and buildable. The demo is immediate: paste messy notes,
get polished launch assets.

## 2. Agent Memory Studio

A local-first markdown knowledge base builder for AI coding agents. It ingests
notes/files/conversation snippets and outputs `AGENTS.md`, `memory/index.md`,
decision logs, and task packs optimized for Codex/Claude/Cursor.

- Impact: 4
- Novelty: 5
- Implementation: 4
- Risk: 2
- Demoability: 4
- Total: 4.25

Why it can work: directly rides the Kanwas/OpenClaw/agent-memory trend and is
very aligned with Codex users. Risk is that the value is slightly more technical
and needs a crisp visual demo.

## 3. Diff-to-Trust Reviewer

Paste a Git diff or PR patch and get an AI-generated review board: risk map,
test gaps, rollback notes, and reviewer-ready comments.

- Impact: 4
- Novelty: 4
- Implementation: 4
- Risk: 2
- Demoability: 5
- Total: 4.20

Why it can work: builds on Kilo Code's diff reviewer signal. Strong for devs, but
crowded unless the angle is "trust before merge" rather than generic code review.

## 4. Review-to-Website Generator

Paste reviews/testimonials for a small business and generate a one-page site
with claims traced to exact customer quotes.

- Impact: 5
- Novelty: 3
- Implementation: 4
- Risk: 3
- Demoability: 5
- Total: 4.05

Why it can work: Brila proves demand. We can build a narrower version without
Google scraping by using pasted reviews, but it may feel derivative.

## 5. Meeting-to-Actions Lite

Paste a transcript and get follow-up emails, tasks, CRM notes, and owner/date
assignments.

- Impact: 4
- Novelty: 3
- Implementation: 5
- Risk: 2
- Demoability: 4
- Total: 4.05

Why it can work: Shadow/Fathom signal is strong, but the category is crowded and
live integrations are out of scope for one day.

## 6. Browser Task Recipe Builder

Record or describe a browser workflow and generate a reusable Playwright-style
recipe plus checklist and expected outputs.

- Impact: 4
- Novelty: 4
- Implementation: 3
- Risk: 4
- Demoability: 5
- Total: 3.70

Why it can work: computer-use/browser automation is hot, but reliable execution
inside a one-day product is riskier than a text/document workflow.
